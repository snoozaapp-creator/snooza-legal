Privacy Policy – Snooza

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

Snooza (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile app, Snooza (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: We use anonymous sign-in; no personal emails or names are required.
  • Sleep Data: Sleep times, wake times, sleep history, and sleep entries.
  • Notifications: Preferences and reminders you enable in the App.
  • Device & Analytics Data: Information collected automatically via Firebase Analytics to improve the App’s performance and usability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App’s features.
  • To track and display your sleep patterns.
  • To send reminders and notifications you opt into.
  • To analyze app usage trends for technical improvements.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your data.
  • Data may be stored and processed by Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Analytics) under their privacy terms.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your anonymous account and associated data by contacting us at snooza.app@gmail.com.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

Snooza is intended for users 13 years or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App and take effect immediately.

8. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, email us at snooza.app@gmail.com
