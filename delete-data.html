Delete Your Data – Snooza

Snooza does not use traditional user accounts.

Your data is associated with an anonymous identifier created on your device. This means we cannot identify you outside of the app.

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete all your data at any time directly داخل the app:

  1. Open the Snooza app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete My Data"

If You Have Already Deleted the App

Because Snooza does not use identifiable accounts, we cannot link data back to you once the app is removed.

Your data will either:

  • Be automatically deleted after a period of time, or
  • Remain stored without any way to associate it with you personally
